Illusions - our daily life VR.

Alonso Ahmetaj
JS Fullstack Web Dev - All things Tech/Writer, Design, Photography passionate.

These days is easy and almost normal to feel lost into some kind of void or loop that is difficult to break through despite the awareness of it. We know this! Yet we don’t apply that knowledge to the circumstances, to the life style and environment around us. We can see this in business, entrepreneurship, politics — Oh yes politics ha.. ha… ehm…in every area that has to do with a human being!

Applied knowledge if that makes sense should be the focus of education these days. Just killing our memories with information that we might not use at all is not the ideal situation you would want to live in. Every one wants to be successful in business, entrepreneurship, life etc who doesn’t but then we do really need to ask ourselves with brutal honesty why is not working?

It is an illusion! The problem lies on the brain’s ability to create a virtual reality that in reality doesn’t coincide with the real world or whatever path one is pursuing. The fact is that everyone knows things! Every one knows what is right or wrong! But we know it with our brains, inside our virtual reality. We lack the need to know it with our actions!

We all have dreams and wants and plans and the list goes to infinity… still going. But the question that stays probably unanswered till you die is…. why is this not working? The only way to break through those self made unconscious realities or illusions is to take action. Hah! Action a word these days that almost makes you feel challenged just by seeing it written.

Feeling real vs real feelings

The reason is difficult to take actions is because of the fear we feel or the comfort we feel in our present circumstances. Another thing we need to realize is that feelings are just feelings! Feelings yes feelings.

We humans live everyday every second in our life based on the way we feel about anything from our thoughts to relationships. Feelings are important since they work as fuel for the brain. They fuel the brain into creating realities about ourselves, daydreams, about the reality around us.

Oh yeah It feels like this therefore must be true or right. However feeling something doesn’t make it true or right. Feelings are real but can those feelings be considered true? We see this with terrorists who kill others because they absolutely feel it is right and thus the only true way. But we know that people who are successful in life, business etc are those who own their feelings. It takes courage to doubt and ask the truth about one’s own feelings no matter how pleasant or how sad or real might those feelings feel.

He who reigns within himself, and rules passions, desires, and fears, is more than a king.

—John Milton

Define them or they will define your reality.

Surely feelings are strong and are meant to be so. I myself I think I might be able to just explain 30% of the feelings I feel on a daily basis and chances are that you too might be part of that majority. It takes a lot of courage, determination and wisdom to see clearly through all the feelings that cross our minds and hearts.

Another obstacle to overcome feelings and break through the self made reality is the lack of ability in understanding our feelings. You probably heard about EQ right?! People with high EQ are considered to be able to describe precisely how they feel. As a result people considered with high EQ are more likely to have control over the way they react in any given situation, be it job interview, decision making or common daily relationships.

The ability to describe properly to someone how you feel is essential in giving feedback and getting the right support and not feeling dumb even after talking to someone for a long time. One good practice that I personally am trying to keep as daily as possible is self checking at the end of the day. Usually I am in my bed just before sleeping. Replaying the whole day in my mind and analyze the feelings and things that I think could or did play an important role in the way that the events unfolded in my day.

Why is this important?

  • First I don’t want to carry with me the feelings I have accumulated during the whole day in my sleep. Trust me it won’t be an easy sleep.
  • Second it helps to feel new start at the time you wake up. Start your day feeling fresh rather than the same.
  • Third you will notice that you will have more control next time you will face some similar situation. Oh yes you will!

The strongest among them all

The spectrum of the feelings we have during a day, month and year is vast and almost out of control. But among the variety of feelings that we feel there is one that beats them all. That is LOVE. Stephen Covey said something wise in his book the 8 habit from effectiveness to greatness that love is not a feeling but an action. Yet the impact or feelings that derives from acting centered on love is enormous in for the whole being.

What makes love the strongest among all is it’s creative attribute. Love has the power to create — from creating your dreams, passions, self reality, family and the list goes on. But what also makes love dangerous is that it is almost impossible to break through it! Wait why would one need to break through it?

Well because yes no matter how amazing love feels there is also false love! Wait I thought love is good and only good! The truth is…..It is not. Wow what are you saying? But love is the purpose of life! If i feel love in whatever I do that means it must be right! Well there is another reality.

Love has a direction. It can be self centered or selfish or it can be centered on for the sake of others. The point is we are constantly loving things just the difference is that we could be loving the wrong way.

The reason we keep doing the same things even though we might know is wrong is because we love it. Love makes us attach strong to anything! Is that wrong? Not at all most of the times is advantageous. However what happens if we attach to the wrong thing?!

That might be a big problem since is difficult to break through it. I love this…I love that… oh yes I want this but in reality I love it this way — is what we keep saying or at least feeling within most of the time without being aware.

I love my business, idea, plan, and so on. Difficult to let it go! Love has a strong connection with the Self! When I love something I become one with it. It is Me. Difficult to let go of My feelings. One of the reasons why love is difficult to deal with is that with love comes pain. Wherever is pain there is love.

The pain we feel in our body is the absence of loving our body properly, the pain we feel in our hearts is the lack of loving others properly. The pain of loneliness is the absence of being loved and loving others. Yet the pain we feel in our body during training is still yet the presence of loving our bodies properly. The pain a mother feels during birth is a result of love.

They say no pain no gain!

Breaking Through

First of all the brutal truth:

You can’t escape self made reality alone!

Guess why? Because the source of the problem and solution is You. The main difficulty is to notice when you are being a problem or a solution. You can’t trust your self….. completely. That means I need a lot of self checks, that is your responsibility.

But you need others just in case you might fool yourself. And yes you got precedents on that… remember? So you need others that help you jump out when you fall into self made illusions again. And yes you are going to fall back again for a while. Be sure to have proper people around.

Successful entrepreneurs, business mans, professionals are not those that just know how to supervise and guide others. Most of them are able to do so because most of them have mastered the ability to check and supervise themselves.

Breaking through the self made illusions in our brains is not easy and the reason why for most of us going the same way for a long time despite knowing it is not right.

This mostly because it is very difficult to let feelings go. To not get attached to some wounds, fears, happiness or joy that might feel real but might not be the path right for us.

Actions are needed to bring us out of our self made realities and those actions are the ones that are not easy. The ones that bring us out of comfort zone.

Because those actions are in a way against yourself. Is a fight with myself and the most difficult person to fight is the self. Therefore one of the most important skills is self checking whether you are true to your principles!
